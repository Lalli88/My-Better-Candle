How We Do It

Candles bring so many good things – relaxation, invigoration, the recall of memory that often comes with scent – but many brands also come with so-called “nasties”: chemical fragrances (which some find induce headaches) and scent boosters, paraffin wax (made from petroleum), which gives off carcinogens when burned, and wicks with a core of metal. Fortunately for candle lovers, there are alternatives. All of our products are made using natural fragrance, such as essential oils, and have pure cotton and/or paper wicks. Our candles are consciously made through and through: they are crafted from a blend of natural wax, hand-poured into our beautiful containers from recycled glass and the boxes we use are 100 per cent recyclable.
