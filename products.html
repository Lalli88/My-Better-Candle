First Step Choose Scent(s)...

Tell us your wishes! What would you like your candle to smell like? Choose from our list or add anything else to your special candle.

Second Step Size and Shape It,

Would you like your candle to represent an elf? Or maybe the face of your dog? We will make it. Would you like the biggest candle ever seen produced? Or perhaps just a very small one? We can do it! NOTE: The smaller the size, the limited the shape.

Third Step Label It!

Write the message that you prefer, stick any picture on your label, or simply leave it blank and we'll fill for you, with as much love and creativity as we put in the candle.

Fourth Step Wait for the Magic...

This is the crucial part of the progress. We will process all the information you provided, try your aromas and put you in contact with one of our experts. Now we tell you if your candle is ready to be produced - not before we sent you a small sample to try it yourself -, if it needs some adjustement because perhaps the final fragrance isn't as lovely as preticted, or if we think another scent or combination added to yours would make the right scent and most important we listen to you; If you have any changes to make, anything to add to or take off from your order this is the perfect moment. Once you received your sample we will ask you for a final confirmation, double check all the details with you and we will send to production the latest information we have received from you.

Finally Enjoy your Creation!

Your candle is finally arrived, we hope you enjoy it and share it with people you love. We would truly appreciate you taking the time to leave us a feedback and/or a comment about your experience with us. We are constantly looking for ways to improve our products and service. Don't forget to tag us on your social media.

Don't trust us? Trust other people who gave it a try and tagged us all over the world.
